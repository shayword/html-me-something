Where I've been

by Shay Throgmartin

  • Arkansas

Arkansas

Arkansas born and raised. I grew up in a small town named Jonesboro, which was surrounded by farms and hordes of mosquitoes. Despite that it was a pleasant town with some good hearted people and a warm glow to it. I started working on my first computers here at the age of 8 and I haven't stoppe since.

Texas

Texas, home of the....George Bush. My father ran the Dupont plants in the south and Texas was Headquarters. I was too young to remember this time in my life but I do know it was very brief. I think we stayed 2 months and moved back to Jonesboro. We were robbed twice during the time. Can't really recommend that.

Arizona

Arizona, what a beautiful place. I moved to Tempe, Arizona in 2004 to attend a college called UAT. I studied Game Design, Project Managment, Network Security, and Programming. It was a very good experience and I made a lot of friends there. I highly recommend anyone to visit this place.

Missouri

Missourri, crazy weather, but not so much different than Arkansas. I moved here at the end of 2016 to attend LaunchCode, actually! So far so good! St. Charles is VERY accomodating and beautiful as well.




Currently in St. Charles, MO.

And here I am, with my beautiful girlfriend Alejandra... where will life take me, well us, next?
